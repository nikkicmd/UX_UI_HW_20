Elsewhere

The need for travel is expected to surge after the pandemic is over, therefore, a more refined and user friendly travel planning mobile app is in demand to help reduce the hassle of ticket booking, hotel booking, route mapping and scheduling. This is a travel application, which allows users to schedule their trips conveniently and efficiently, by providing useful information or suggesting itineraries at destinations. Helps the user manage time, attractions, budget and expense during the journey

Background

Problem

Users have to jump from 2-3 different apps just to plan out their travel lending them into a confusing process of booking and mistakes unable to make a custom itinerary and have to stick to the ones provided by the agency when it comes to truly having control over ones travel plan and schedule

Solution

Elsewhere will help manage all your trip details in one platform giving you the freedom to create trips and itineraries to the user's liking. Taking all the hasele of booking and making it quick and easy to access.



Research

Qualitative Research

I interviewed 5 potential users over the zoom to understand the challenges they face when travelling.

Some of the questions I asked:
1. How does a user plan their itinerary on a vacation?
2. What platforms are they using to book their travel flights and accommodations?
3. What are the pain points a user comes across when booking their vacation?
4. What types of users will use this app?
5. What influences a user on where to travel
6. Are users feeling safe about travelling post pandemic
7. Do users enjoy using a travel agency or a online book service

User Insight Statement

Amy is a young professional, with a desire to travel and elevate her photography portfolio. This summer she is planning on taking a 3 week European vacation with her boyfriend. She has a lot of places she would like to see so it’s important for her to stay organized so that she doesn’t feel overwhelmed while on vacation. During the research process I discovered that many users felt overwhelmed with having to organize all their travel dates, hotel stays, activities and ticket purchases. It was hard to have everything organized in one place and easily accessible on day of travel. I believe that people could benefit from having an app that will help manage all their trip details in one place so you’ll no longer need to sort through tons of emails to find that email reservation, or you’ll be able to determine which days you forgot to book a hotel.

User Persona

The user group is target for the age group of 18-40 years old. The millennial generation which loves to explore, travel, discover and have a community of ties formed at the end of their trip
